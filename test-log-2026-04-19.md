- **File:** `packages/domain/src/service.ts`
- **Added:** `detectCycle()` DFS helper (WHITE/GRAY/BLACK 3-colour marking)
- **Called in:** `createCharter` — runs before any DB writes, immediately after `assertTaskWeights` and `assertBaselineSplit`

### BUG-03 Fixed — `claimTask` `SELECT … FOR UPDATE`
- **File:** `packages/domain/src/service.ts`
- **Function:** `claimTask`
- **Added:** `SELECT status FROM charter_tasks WHERE … FOR UPDATE` immediately after the soft status check, inside the same `withClient` transaction
- **Effect:** Concurrent agents racing to claim the same task are serialised at the DB level. The second transaction blocks on the lock, then re-reads status (now `CLAIMED`) and throws before reaching the `UPDATE`, preventing double-claim
